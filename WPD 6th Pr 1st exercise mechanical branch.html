MECHANICAL ENGINEERING

ABOUT DEPARTMENT

The course leading to a diploma in Mechanical Engineering which was started in the year of 1956 with intake of 60 students. Today the department has total intake capacity of 180 including 60 intake for Second Shift(Minority) which started in the year 2011.
Mechanical Engineering Covers:Engineering Drawing, Theory Of Machines, Fluid Mechanics and Machines, Thermal Engineering, Manufacturing Processes, Design Of Machine Elements, Industrial Fluid Power, Solid Modeling
